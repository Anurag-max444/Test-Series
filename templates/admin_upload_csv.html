📋 CSV Format

Your CSV must have these columns in this exact order:

class_name,subject,chapter,question,option_a,option_b,option_c,option_d,correct_option,explanation

Example row:

10th,Physics,Motion,What is speed?,Fast,Slow,Distance/Time,None,C,Speed = Distance ÷ Time
  • correct_option must be exactly: A, B, C, or D
  • class_name must be: 10th, 11th, or 12th
  • subject must be: Physics, Chemistry, or Math
  • explanation column is optional but recommended
  • Include a header row (column names on first line)
  • Rows with errors will be skipped silently
